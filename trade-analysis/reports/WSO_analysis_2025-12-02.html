Company Overview

Sector: Industrials | Industry: Industrial Distribution

CEO: Albert H. Nahmad | Founded: 1945

Headquarters: Miami, Florida

Watsco, Inc., together with its subsidiaries, is the largest distributor of air conditioning, heating, refrigeration equipment, and related parts and supplies in North America. The company serves contractors and dealers that service the replacement and new construction markets for residential and light commercial central air conditioning, heating, and refrigeration systems. As of December 31, 2021, it operated from 671 locations in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and Puerto Rico.

Key Business Segments:
  • Residential ducted and ductless air conditioning systems
  • Gas, electric, and oil furnaces
  • Commercial HVAC equipment systems
  • Replacement parts and components
  • Ancillary supplies and tools
