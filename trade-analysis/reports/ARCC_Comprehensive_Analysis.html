Business Model

Ares Capital is a market-leading Business Development Company (BDC) and one of the largest direct lenders in the U.S. The company provides financing solutions to middle-market companies, typically with annual EBITDA between $10M and $250M. As a BDC, ARCC must distribute at least 90% of taxable income as dividends to maintain favorable tax treatment.
