## üéØ 5-Factor Backtest Methodology

### Scoring System (Total: 100 points)

1. **Gap Size (25 points)**
   - Measures post-earnings price reaction
   - Larger gaps indicate stronger market conviction
   - 10%+ gaps score highest

2. **Pre-Earnings Trend (30 points)** - HIGHEST WEIGHT
   - Analyzes 30-60 day trend before earnings
   - Stocks in uptrends tend to continue post-earnings
   - Momentum continuation principle

3. **Volume Trend (20 points)**
   - Relative volume vs 90-day average
   - Institutional participation indicator
   - 1.5x+ relative volume scores highest

4. **MA200 Position (15 points)**
   - Long-term trend health indicator
   - Above MA200 = bullish structure
   - Distance from MA200 shows trend strength

5. **MA50 Position (10 points)**
   - Medium-term momentum indicator
   - Confirms intermediate trend direction
   - Proximity to price shows support/resistance

### Grade Classifications

- **A-Grade (85-100 pts):** 70%+ historical win rate | High conviction | 2-3% position size
- **B-Grade (70-84 pts):** 55-69% win rate | Moderate conviction | 1-2% position size
- **C-Grade (55-69 pts):** 40-54% win rate | Speculative | <1% position size
- **D-Grade (<55 pts):** <40% win rate | Avoid or short candidates
