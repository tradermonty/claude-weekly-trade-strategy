## Posting Strategy Recommendations:

1. **Timing**: Post during market hours or immediately after close (4-6 PM ET) for maximum engagement

2. **Thread Option**: Consider posting as a thread with:
   - Tweet 1: Summary with A-grade picks
   - Tweet 2: Detailed scores and technical analysis
   - Tweet 3: Risk disclaimer and full report link

3. **Engagement Tactics**:
   - Use relevant hashtags but don't overdo it (3-5 max)
   - Tag relevant accounts if appropriate ($CIFR has major news, could tag @AWS if company mentioned it)
   - Include chart/screenshot from the HTML report for visual engagement
   - Ask question at end to drive comments: "Which earnings play do you like best?"

4. **Follow-up Posts**:
   - Update progress on these trades in 2-3 days
   - Share which predictions were accurate
   - Build credibility with transparent tracking

5. **Compliance Note**:
   - Always include risk disclaimer
   - Don't claim guaranteed returns
   - State these are educational ideas, not investment advice
   - Consider adding: "Not financial advice. DYOR."
