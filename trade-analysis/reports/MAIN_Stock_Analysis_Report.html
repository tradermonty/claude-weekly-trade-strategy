Company Overview

Main Street Capital Corporation is a business development company (BDC) specializing in equity and debt capital investments in lower middle market companies. Founded in 2007 and headquartered in Houston, Texas, MAIN focuses on companies with annual revenues between $5 million and $300 million, providing one-stop financing alternatives for management buyouts, recapitalizations, refinancing, and growth financing. The company invests across diverse sectors including financial services, health care, technology, consumer services, and industrial services.
