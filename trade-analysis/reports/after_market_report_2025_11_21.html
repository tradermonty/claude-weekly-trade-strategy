U.S. Stock Market Analysis

November 21, 2025 - Final After-Market Data

Executive Summary

U.S. markets rebounded strongly on Friday as investors shook off the previous day's tech-led selloff following NVIDIA's earnings. The Russell 2000 (IWM) led the charge with an impressive +2.83% gain, signaling renewed risk appetite in small-cap stocks. The S&P 500 rose +1.00% while the Dow Jones added +0.98%. The NASDAQ recovered with +0.75% despite continued pressure on semiconductor names. The VIX retreated from yesterday's spike, falling to 23.43 (down 11.3%), indicating declining fear levels. Healthcare (+2.06%) and Communication Services (+1.88%) led sector performance, while Technology lagged at +0.12%. Notable volume surge activity was detected in 267 stocks, with retail earnings from ROST and GAP driving strong moves. Eli Lilly made history by becoming the first pharmaceutical company to reach a $1 trillion market cap.
